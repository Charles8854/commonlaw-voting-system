Ballot: USA: Presidential Candidates, Preferential-Voting

Instructions:

This Ballot is a "Template" for general & public use. In their Response To This Ballot, Registered Users in our Common-Law Republic May Submit Their Votes For Immediate & Real-Time Tallying By Our Computer System.

Communities Other than our Common-Law Republic Group May Process & Tally Votes From Ballots Similar As This. This Technology Is Public, Open-Source, & Non-Proprietary.

Users of this Ballot May Modify It by Adding or Subtracting Names to the Long List of Candidates Presented Here-In. Subtracted Listed Names will simply Not Be Counted in Polling Vote-Tallying Results. Added Names will be Subjected to Approval from Common-Law Republic Administrative Staff.

Registered Users should Modify Their Version of This Ballot by Inserting Numbers Closer to 100 For Preferred-Candidates, & by Inserting Numbers Closer to Zero for Opposed-Candidates.
